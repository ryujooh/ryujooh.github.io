# The Art of Less

In an age of constant noise, **minimalism** is not about having less; it is about making room for more of what matters.

## Design Philosophy

- Extreme simplicity.
- Black and White.
- Thin Typography.
- Purposeful whitespace.

This blog is a testament to this philosophy.
